Lessons are given several days during the week and the weekend, depending on your municipality and the age of your child. This information will be communicated to you during registration.

Turn Your Kid into a Genius !!!

Our course is intended to develop in a synchronous and harmonious way, the work of the two cerebral hemispheres, by a series of exercises using an abacus and with a proven method. It’s not just about calculating but rather about training your child to use brain resources that kid doesn’t usually activate. We will train your kid in fast calculation, memory activation, puzzle solving, mental and physical synchronization, and many other applications.
